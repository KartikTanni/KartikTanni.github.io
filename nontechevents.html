Face Painting

About

Bored of painting on paper? Try something different! Magnovite brings you a great opportunity to show your creativity. Face painting is an exciting, fun event bringing a splash of colour and a sprinkle of glitter to brighten up the atmosphere. Painting a canvas is tricky, a face even more. Come, wow us with your creativity and stand a chance to win exciting cash prizes!

Rules and guidelines

  • It is a team event so participants will have to get their own volunteers (one individual on whom face painting will be done).
  • Theme for the face painting competition will be given on the spot.
  • Time allotted is 1 hour.
  • Participants have to get their own required materials for face painting.
  • No exchange of materials between the participants will be allowed.
  • No reference material will be allowed once the competition has begun.
  • Participants who are late will not be given extra time.
  • Participants with cold sore, eczema, open cut, conjunctivitis are requested not to participate.
  • Participants found breaking the rules will be disqualified.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity
  • Clarity in terms of using colours and differences in colours, shades, strokes, etc. according to the theme given.
  • Design
  • Explanation about the painting.

General Instructions

  • The event will be cancelled if the number of participants is less than 10 teams.
  • Students should carry their college ID cards.

Registration Details

  • Registration fees Rs-100/- per team.
  • Min-10 teams
  • Max-50 teams

  • 3rd Prize Rs. 1K
